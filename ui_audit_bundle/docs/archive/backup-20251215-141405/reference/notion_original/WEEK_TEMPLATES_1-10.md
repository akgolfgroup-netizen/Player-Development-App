# Week Plan Templates 1-10
## AK Golf Academy × Team Norway Golf

**Purpose**: Foundation weekly templates covering primary categories and periods
**Coverage**: Categories C-G (most common), all 4 periods
**Version**: 1.0

---

## Template Structure

Each weekly template includes:
- **7-day training schedule** (Mon-Sun)
- **Session assignments** (linked to TRENINGSØKTER database)
- **Training distribution** breakdown
- **Total hours** per week
- **Rest day** designation
- **Customization** notes

## Implementation Guide

### How to Apply Templates

**Step 1: Select Template**
- Match player category (A-K)
- Match current training period
- Choose variant based on:
  - **Standard**: Default for most players
  - **Intensiv**: High commitment, elite players
  - **Lett**: Injury recovery, school exams, young players

**Step 2: Customize for Player**
- Adjust days based on school/work schedule
- Swap sessions based on facility availability
- Modify rest day placement
- Consider player's breaking points (add targeted drills)

**Step 3: Link to PERIODISERING**
- Assign template to specific weeks in player's 52-week plan
- Templates can repeat or rotate
- Example: Weeks 47-50 use Template 1, Weeks 51-2 use Template 1 (Intensiv)

**Step 4: Monitor and Adjust**
- Track session completion
- Adjust if player shows fatigue or staleness
- Swap to "Lett" variant if needed
- Modify distribution based on breaking points
