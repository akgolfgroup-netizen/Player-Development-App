## General Misuse

### DO NOT

#### 1. Create Unofficial Variations
- No "fun" versions of the logo
- No seasonal modifications without approval
- No combining with other logos

#### 2. Use Brand Assets for Non-TIER Golf Purposes
- No personal projects
- No third-party products
- No merchandise without approval

#### 3. Modify Brand Elements
- No creating new color variations
- No custom icon styles
- No altered typography

## Reporting Brand Misuse

If you encounter brand misuse:
1. Document with screenshots
2. Note the location/context
3. Report to the design team

*When in doubt, refer to this guide or contact the design team.*
