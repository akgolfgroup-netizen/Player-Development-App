## SESSION 1: Teknikk - Driver Fundamentals - L2_S1

**Categories**: D, E, F, G
**Type**: Teknikk - Full Swing
**Period**: Grunnperiode
**Duration**: 60 min
**L-fase**: L2 (Fundamentals)
**Setting**: S1 (Range, perfect conditions)
**CS-nivå**: CS50 (Comfort zone)

**Objectives**:
- Primary: Establish proper driver setup and takeaway
- Secondary: Build awareness of clubface control

**Structure**:
1. **Warmup** (10 min)
   - Joint mobility (hips, shoulders, thoracic)
   - Practice swings with alignment stick
   - 5 wedge swings @ CS40

2. **Main Work** (35 min)
   - Exercise 1: Setup position check (5 min)
   - Exercise 2: Takeaway to chest-high drill (10 min)
   - Exercise 3: Half swings with feedback (10 min)
   - Exercise 4: Full swings, video review every 5 swings (10 min)

3. **Transfer Practice** (10 min)
   - 10 drives to different targets
   - Focus: Apply fundamentals to variable targets

4. **Cooldown** (5 min)
   - Stretch routine
   - Review video footage

**Equipment**: Driver, alignment sticks, video camera, launch monitor (optional)

**Success Criteria**:
- Setup consistency >80% (coach check)
- Takeaway path on-plane 7/10 swings
- Player can identify own setup issues

**Notes**: If struggling with setup, spend extra time on positioning before moving to swing

## SESSION 2: Putting - Lag Control - L3_S2

**Categories**: C, D, E, F
**Type**: Putting - Lag
**Period**: Grunnperiode, Spesialiseringsperiode
**Duration**: 45 min
**L-fase**: L3 (Variasjon)
**Setting**: S2 (Range, structured practice)
**CS-nivå**: N/A (putting)

**Objectives**:
- Primary: Develop distance control 3-15 meters
- Secondary: Build feel for green speed variations

**Structure**:
1. **Warmup** (5 min)
   - Wrist rolls
   - Practice stroke without ball
   - 10 putts @ 2m for feel

2. **Main Work** (30 min)
   - Exercise 1: 3-6-9 meter drill (10 min)
     * 6 putts each distance, goal: all within 3 feet
   - Exercise 2: Random distance drill (10 min)
     * Coach calls distances 4-12m, player responds
   - Exercise 3: Ladder drill (10 min)
     * 3m, 6m, 9m, 12m, 15m, back down

3. **Pressure Test** (8 min)
   - 5 attempts: Make 3 consecutive lag putts inside 3 feet

4. **Cooldown** (2 min)
   - Short putts for confidence (2m, make 5)

**Equipment**: Putter, 15+ balls, distance markers

**Success Criteria**:
- 70%+ putts finish within 3 feet (3-9m range)
- 50%+ putts within 3 feet (9-15m range)
- Complete pressure test at least once

**Notes**: Adjust distances for green speed. Fast greens: reduce max distance.

## SESSION 3: Fysisk - Rotational Power - Gym

**Categories**: C, D, E, F, G
**Type**: Fysisk - Eksplosivitet
**Period**: Grunnperiode, Spesialiseringsperiode
**Duration**: 50 min
**L-fase**: L2 (Fundamentals)
**Setting**: Gym
**CS-nivå**: N/A

**Objectives**:
- Primary: Develop rotational power for golf swing
- Secondary: Build core stability and control

**Structure**:
1. **Warmup** (10 min)
   - Dynamic stretching
   - Core activation (planks, side planks)
   - Light medicine ball rotations

2. **Main Work** (30 min)
   - Exercise 1: Medicine ball rotational throws (4kg) - 3×8 each side
   - Exercise 2: Cable chops (high to low) - 3×10 each side
   - Exercise 3: Landmine rotations - 3×12 each side
   - Exercise 4: Pallof press - 3×15 each side
   - Exercise 5: Rotational jumps - 2×10

3. **Golf-specific Transfer** (8 min)
   - Weighted club swings (overspeed training)
   - Power swings with light club

4. **Cooldown** (2 min)
   - Static stretching
   - Foam rolling

**Equipment**: Medicine ball (4kg), cable machine, landmine attachment, resistance bands, weighted club

**Success Criteria**:
- Med ball throw distance: Track baseline for improvement
- Maintain proper form throughout all sets
- No lower back pain or discomfort

**Notes**: Critical - Proper form over heavy weight. Progress load only when form is perfect.

## SESSION 4: Shortgame - Wedge Distance Control - L3_S2

**Categories**: D, E, F, G
**Type**: Teknikk - Wedge
**Period**: Grunnperiode, Spesialiseringsperiode
**Duration**: 60 min
**L-fase**: L3 (Variasjon)
**Setting**: S2 (Shortgame area, structured)
**CS-nivå**: CS60

**Objectives**:
- Primary: Master 4 distinct wedge distances
- Secondary: Improve wedge PEI score

**Structure**:
1. **Warmup** (10 min)
   - Chip shots with various clubs
   - Feel finder: Hit to different distances

2. **Main Work - Clock System** (35 min)
   - Exercise 1: 9:00 swing = X meters (identify distance)
   - Exercise 2: 10:30 swing = Y meters
   - Exercise 3: 12:00 swing = Z meters
   - Exercise 4: Full swing = Max meters
   - For each: 10 balls, measure average distance ± deviation

3. **Variability Practice** (10 min)
   - Random targets at identified distances
   - Goal: Dial in correct swing length

4. **Cooldown Test** (5 min)
   - Mini PEI test: 6 shots to flag, measure accuracy

**Equipment**: 3 wedges (SW, GW, PW), laser rangefinder, targets

**Success Criteria**:
- Identify 4 consistent distance windows
- Standard deviation <5m for each distance
- PEI test: Average within 6m of target

**Notes**: Record distance windows in player notes for future reference

## SESSION 5: Mental - Pre-shot Routine Building - L2_S1

**Categories**: All (C-K)
**Type**: Mental - Rutiner
**Period**: Grunnperiode
**Duration**: 45 min
**L-fase**: L2 (Fundamentals)
**Setting**: S1 (Range, controlled environment)
**CS-nivå**: CS50

**Objectives**:
- Primary: Establish consistent pre-shot routine
- Secondary: Build timing and rhythm awareness

**Structure**:
1. **Education** (10 min)
   - Explain importance of routines
   - Show pro examples (video)
   - Discuss elements: Physical + Mental

2. **Routine Design** (15 min)
   - Player creates personal routine with coach
   - Elements to include:
     * Behind ball view (2-3 sec)
     * Practice swing (1)
     * Approach to ball (3-4 steps)
     * Final look at target (1 sec)
     * Trigger and swing
   - Time total routine (aim for 15-20 sec)

3. **Practice Implementation** (15 min)
   - Hit 20 balls using routine
   - Film first 5, middle 5, last 5
   - Review for consistency

4. **Evaluation** (5 min)
   - Review footage
   - Measure timing variation
   - Adjust if needed

**Equipment**: Clubs, balls, video camera, stopwatch

**Success Criteria**:
- Routine timing ±2 seconds consistency (18/20 shots)
- Player can verbally describe routine steps
- Routine feels natural (player assessment)

**Notes**: Routine will evolve - This is v1.0, expect refinements

## SESSION 6: Teknikk - Iron Striking - L2_S1

**Categories**: D, E, F
**Type**: Teknikk - Full Swing
**Period**: Grunnperiode
**Duration**: 60 min
**L-fase**: L2 (Fundamentals)
**Setting**: S1 (Range, mats okay)
**CS-nivå**: CS50

**Objectives**:
- Primary: Solid contact with irons (compression)
- Secondary: Improve ball-first contact consistency

**Structure**:
1. **Warmup** (10 min)
   - Mobility drills
   - Half wedge swings for feel

2. **Main Work** (35 min)
   - Exercise 1: Impact bag work - Feel compression (5 min)
   - Exercise 2: Towel drill - Ball-first contact (10 min)
     * Place towel 6 inches behind ball, must miss towel
   - Exercise 3: Low point control drill (10 min)
     * Tee barely in ground, focus on taking divot after ball
   - Exercise 4: Quality strikes tracking (10 min)
     * Hit 20 x 7-iron, rate each 1-5 on contact quality
     * Track stats: Solid (4-5), Average (3), Poor (1-2)

3. **Transfer** (10 min)
   - Different club each shot (6i, 7i, 8i, 9i)
   - Maintain same swing quality

4. **Cooldown** (5 min)
   - Easy wedges
   - Review strike quality stats

**Equipment**: Irons (6-9), impact bag, towel, tees, launch monitor (helpful for spin tracking)

**Success Criteria**:
- 70%+ solid contact rating (4-5)
- Divot after ball 15/20 swings
- Consistent ball flight (height and shape)

**Notes**: If struggling, reduce to 9-iron only until pattern established

## SESSION 7: Spill - Bane Simulation - L4_S4

**Categories**: C, D, E
**Type**: Spill - Range
**Period**: Spesialiseringsperiode, Turneringsperiode
**Duration**: 90 min
**L-fase**: L4 (Timing and Flow)
**Setting**: S4 (9-hole practice round)
**CS-nivå**: CS70 (Competitive speed)

**Objectives**:
- Primary: Simulate tournament conditions on course
- Secondary: Practice shot selection and strategy

**Structure**:
1. **Warmup** (20 min)
   - Full warmup as if tournament day
   - Putting: 10 min
   - Range: 10 min (short to long)

2. **9-Hole Simulation** (60 min)
   - Play 9 holes with specific constraints:
     * Use pre-shot routine every shot
     * Make all putts inside 3 feet
     * Track Strokes Gained categories mentally
   - Walk like tournament pace
   - Between-shot routine practice

3. **Post-Round Review** (10 min)
   - Analyze:
     * Pre-shot routine consistency
     * Shot selection quality
     * Mental state management
     * Keys for improvement

**Equipment**: Full bag, scorecard, routine checklist

**Success Criteria**:
- Pre-shot routine 15/18+ shots
- Strategic shot selection (coach evaluation)
- Maintain focus throughout 9 holes
- Score within 3 shots of handicap-adjusted par

**Notes**: This is PRACTICE not scoring - Focus on process over result

## SESSION 8: Putting - Green Reading - L3_S2

**Categories**: C, D, E, F
**Type**: Putting - Lesing
**Period**: Spesialiseringsperiode
**Duration**: 50 min
**L-fase**: L3 (Variasjon)
**Setting**: S2 (Putting green, multiple slopes)
**CS-nivå**: N/A

**Objectives**:
- Primary: Improve green reading accuracy
- Secondary: Build aim-point or plumb-bob skill

**Structure**:
1. **Education** (10 min)
   - Green reading fundamentals
   - Slope assessment techniques
   - Aim-point method introduction OR plumb-bob

2. **Straight Putt Baseline** (5 min)
   - Find flat area
   - 10 putts @ 3m to establish baseline make %

3. **Reading Practice** (25 min)
   - Exercise 1: Uphill/Downhill (8 min)
     * 4 putts uphill, 4 downhill from 3m
     * Predict speed needed, execute
   - Exercise 2: Breaking Putts - Left/Right (12 min)
     * Find 6 different breaking putts
     * For each: Predict break, hit putt, measure actual break
     * Track prediction accuracy
   - Exercise 3: Double-breakers (5 min)
     * Find complex putts
     * Use reading system to predict path

4. **Reading Test** (8 min)
   - 6 random putts (coach selects)
   - Player must:
     * Call break direction and amount
     * Aim accordingly
     * Execute
   - Score: Correct reads /6

5. **Cooldown** (2 min)
   - Short straight putts

**Equipment**: Putter, balls, aim-point chart OR plumb-bob reference

**Success Criteria**:
- Reading test: 4/6 correct predictions
- Improved awareness of slope influence
- Can verbalize reading process

**Notes**: Reading system is personal - Find what works for player

## SESSION 10: Shortgame - Bunker Play - L2_S2

**Categories**: D, E, F, G
**Type**: Shortgame - Bunker
**Period**: Grunnperiode, Spesialiseringsperiode
**Duration**: 50 min
**L-fase**: L2 (Fundamentals)
**Setting**: S2 (Practice bunker)
**CS-nivå**: CS60

**Objectives**:
- Primary: Establish consistent bunker technique
- Secondary: Improve bunker test score (Test 7: 10 shots, % within 3m)

**Structure**:
1. **Setup and Technique Review** (10 min)
   - Stance: Open, ball forward
   - Clubface: Open
   - Entry point: 2 inches behind ball
   - Demonstration and feel

2. **Progressive Drills** (30 min)
   - Exercise 1: Line in sand drill (10 min)
     * Draw line, practice hitting 2" behind without ball
     * Add ball when consistent
   - Exercise 2: Distance control (10 min)
     * 3 distances: Short (5m), Medium (10m), Long (15m)
     * Learn swing length for each
   - Exercise 3: Lie variations (10 min)
     * Buried lie technique
     * Uphill/downhill lies
     * Practice each 5 shots

3. **Bunker Test Simulation** (8 min)
   - 10 shots from good lie
   - Measure: % within 3m (Test 7 protocol)
   - Track baseline score

4. **Cooldown** (2 min)
   - Final 5 easy shots for confidence

**Equipment**: Sand wedge, rake, measuring tape, balls

**Success Criteria**:
- Get ball out of bunker 10/10 times
- Test 7 score: 40%+ within 3m (beginner), 60%+ (intermediate)
- Consistent entry point 2" behind ball

**Notes**: Most common error: Trying to "scoop" rather than hitting sand
