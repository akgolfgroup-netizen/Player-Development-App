# Schedule A – Pricing & Packaging

## Binding Principles
- Unit: **Active Player per Month**
- Contract Term: **12 months (annual commitment)**
- Billing: **Monthly**
- Pricing: **Standard only – no custom pricing in Phase 1**

## Definition – Active Player
A player is considered *Active* if at least one of the following occurred in the last 30 days:
- Completed a structured session
- Confirmed a weekly plan
- Submitted training logs or feedback
